InkCue Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

English

InkCue stores supply names, categories, printer/model notes, cartridge codes, colors, quantities, unit labels, low-stock thresholds, storage locations, review dates, cycles, reminder lead times, warranty or expiry dates, notes, completion counts, missed counts, and recent activity on your device and in the App Group container used by widgets.

Notification permission is optional and used only for local printer supply reminders. Reminders access is optional and used only after you choose to sync dated supply checks to Apple Reminders.

Free users may see clearly labeled Google AdMob native ads. Plus subscribers stay ad-free. Depending on your ATT choice and device settings, Google may process ad identifiers, device information, and ad interaction data to deliver and measure ads. InkCue does not create accounts, use cloud sync, connect to printer accounts, scan networks, read live ink levels, buy supplies automatically, verify genuine cartridges, fix printer errors, or claim affiliation with printer brands or retailers.

通知权限是可选的，只用于本地打印耗材提醒。提醒事项权限也是可选的，只在你选择把带日期的耗材复查同步到 Apple 提醒事项后使用。

免费用户可能看到明确标注的 Google AdMob 原生广告。Plus 订阅用户无广告。根据你的 ATT 选择和设备设置，Google 可能处理广告标识符、设备信息和广告互动数据以投放和衡量广告。InkCue 不创建账号、不使用云同步、不连接打印机账号、不扫描网络、不读取实时墨量、不自动购买耗材、不验证正品耗材、不修复打印机错误，也不声称与打印机品牌或零售商有关联。
