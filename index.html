InkCue Support

Reminder Boundary

InkCue uses local notifications and optional Apple Reminders sync after you turn them on. It does not write to Calendar, iPhone Clock, or system alarms, and it does not read live printer levels, scan networks, connect to printer accounts, or buy supplies automatically.
